How it works

Three steps to
smarter decisions

01

Pick a category & set your criteria

Choose what you're looking for — property, cars, jobs or holidays. Each category opens a purpose-built search form tailored to that market. Set your location, budget, radius, and any must-haves.

  • Property: bedrooms, price range, radius, parking & garden
  • Cars: make, model, mileage, fuel type & transmission
  • Jobs: title, location, salary range & remote preference
  • Searches Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket & more simultaneously
crabify.com — Search
02

Crabify searches every site for you

Hit search and Crabify scrapes every relevant listing site at once — pulling real-time results across all sources into a single, unified grid. No more switching between tabs.

  • Results from multiple sources in one place
  • Each card shows source badge, price, specs & description
  • Filter, sort and compare side-by-side
  • New listings flagged automatically on repeat searches
crabify.com — Results
03

Download, share or act on your results

Every search produces a structured report with prices, photos, specs and contact details. Export it as a CSV, share it with a link, or contact agents and sellers directly — all without leaving Crabify.

  • Download results as CSV for offline analysis
  • Share a results link with a partner or advisor
  • Contact agents directly via phone or viewing request
  • Re-run your search weekly or daily to catch new listings

Why Crabify

Built for buyers who
value their time

Saves you hours

Instead of opening ten browser tabs and comparing listings manually, Crabify does the leg work in seconds — aggregating, ranking, and summarising results for you.

No more missed listings

Crabify searches Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket, AutoTrader, Reed, Indeed, and more simultaneously — so nothing slips through the cracks.

Clean, exportable reports

Every search produces a structured summary with prices, photos, specs, and contact details — ready to share, save, or export as a PDF.

Tailored to each category

Property searches filter by bedrooms and radius. Car searches filter by make, mileage, and price. Each category has a purpose-built search form — not a generic one.

Always up to date

Crabify re-runs your searches on a schedule — daily, weekly, or monthly depending on your plan — and flags new listings that match your criteria.

Private by design

Your search criteria and results are yours alone. Crabify does not sell your data or share your preferences with listing sites or third-party advertisers.
